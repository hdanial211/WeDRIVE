Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting with WeDRIVE.

What do I need to rent a car? expand_more
You'll need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name for the security deposit, and an international driving permit if your license is not in English or Malay.
How does the Smart Availability work? expand_more
Our intelligent system tracks real-time return statuses and maintenance schedules. If you see a car available on your dates, it is guaranteed to be ready for you—no double bookings, no last-minute substitutions.
Can I cancel or modify my reservation? expand_more
Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking through the app. Cancellations made 24 hours prior to pickup are fully refundable. Modifications are subject to current car availability and pricing.
Where do I pick up the car in Melaka? expand_more
During the 'Seamless Booking' step, you can select from multiple convenient hubs across Melaka, including the city center and transit stations. Detailed directions and exact bay numbers are provided in your app itinerary.
