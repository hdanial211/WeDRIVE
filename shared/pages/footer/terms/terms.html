2. Payment Terms

Payment is required at the time of booking. We accept Visa, Mastercard, FPX, and e-wallets.

  • A refundable security deposit of RM 500-2,000 applies depending on vehicle class
  • Deposit is released within 7 business days after return
  • Late returns incur a charge of 50% of the daily rate per additional hour
